My Bookings

Manage your lessons

Once logged in, you can:
  • View all your upcoming and past lessons.
  • Change or delete reservations (24h in advance).
  • Check your remaining lesson balance.

How it works

1

Sign in

Click Register or Sign in in the top right of the calendar.

2

Open Agenda view

Click the Week dropdown and select Agenda.

3

Manage Reservations

View upcoming lessons and history.

4

Edit Reservation

Click the blue pencil icon to edit.

5

Cancel / Delete

Click the red trash icon to cancel.
